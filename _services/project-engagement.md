## What it is

A Project Engagement is a structured, scoped engagement for organizations ready to redesign one or more platforms or workflows. It combines discovery, design, and delivery with clear milestones — and ends with work that your engineering team can build from.

## Who it's for

- Organizations that have completed a Discovery Sprint and are ready to act on the findings
- Companies redesigning a B2B SaaS product, an internal tool, or a frontline worker application
- Fintech and regulated companies rebuilding a product within compliance constraints
- Enterprise teams that need a senior design partner to lead a bounded redesign without managing a full agency

## Scope

A Project Engagement typically covers:

- **1 or 2 platforms** — not the entire product surface
- **One primary user group** — with secondary users documented but not fully designed for
- **6–12 weeks** — depending on scope, complexity, and stakeholder availability

Larger scope (3+ platforms, 3+ user groups) converts to an Embedded Retainer rather than a Project Engagement.

## What you get

- Research synthesis and discovery findings (if not preceded by a Sprint)
- UX design across the agreed scope — wireframes, flows, prototypes
- Service blueprint and workflow design for cross-platform coherence
- Usability testing with real users (minimum 5 participants)
- Design system foundations — component documentation, patterns, spacing and type decisions
- A developer handoff package — annotated specs, interaction notes, asset exports

## Pricing

**$18,000** for a 6-week engagement covering one platform with moderate complexity

**$35,000** for a 12-week engagement covering two platforms with high complexity, regulated constraints, or significant stakeholder coordination
