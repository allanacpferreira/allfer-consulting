## What it is

An Embedded Retainer is for organizations that need a senior design partner embedded in their product team over time — without the overhead of a full-time hire.

For ~60 hours per month, you get continuous senior UX expertise: design, research, stakeholder alignment, and strategy — all integrated into your existing product process.

## Who it's for

- Enterprise SaaS companies with a product team but no senior UX leadership
- Organizations that completed a Project Engagement and want to maintain design quality as they build
- Companies undergoing sustained AI transformation who need a design partner present throughout
- Regulated industries where UX quality is an ongoing compliance consideration, not a one-time project

## What 60 hours covers

In a typical month:

- Active design work on 1–2 product areas
- Weekly sync with product lead or CTO (1 hour)
- User research sessions as needed (typically 2–4 per month)
- Design QA on engineering handoffs
- Ad hoc stakeholder alignment calls
- Monthly strategy review and forward planning

## What you get

- A senior design partner who knows your product, your users, and your constraints — without restarting from zero each month
- Continuity across sprints and roadmap cycles
- A trusted advisor who can raise design risks before they become expensive to fix
- Monthly strategy review that connects design work to business outcomes

## Minimum Commitment

3 months. Most retainer relationships run 6–12 months. The value of an embedded retainer increases substantially as institutional knowledge accumulates — the first month is the least efficient; month 6 is the most.

## Pricing

**$10,000/month** for organizations with 1 active platform and a product team that is already design-literate

**$14,000/month** for organizations with multiple active surfaces, higher stakeholder complexity, or regulated products requiring additional documentation

Payment terms: Monthly in advance.
