## Context

Safrapay is a fintech company offering financial services to business clients. The ISO compliance portal served as the primary interface for regulated compliance workflows — used by business clients to manage their documentation, status, and compliance obligations.

## Challenge

Designing a compliance portal requires navigating a fundamental tension: regulatory requirements impose information architecture and disclosure constraints that frequently conflict with usability best practices. The portal had to serve users who were not UX-literate, in a context where errors carried regulatory consequences.

## Role

UX Designer → UX Team Lead. This engagement was completed prior to founding Allfer Consulting, during the Chairman's tenure at Safrapay.
