## Context

First Student operates one of the largest school transportation networks in North America. Their frontline workforce — tens of thousands of bus drivers — required a digital product that worked under real operational conditions: time pressure, physical context, variable connectivity, and shift-based workflows.

## Challenge

Frontline worker applications fail for predictable reasons: designed by people who have never used the product under real conditions, in a context where errors have immediate physical consequences. The Driver app needed to work for a workforce that is not digitally sophisticated, using devices with variable capability, in situations where the app was a tool — not an experience.

## Role

UX Designer. Owned the onboarding domain end-to-end across mobile and web. This engagement was completed prior to founding Allfer Consulting.
