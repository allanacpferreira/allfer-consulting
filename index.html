Four Capability Pillars

What we do — and why it's hard.

Each pillar is a distinct competency, grounded in real engagements. Together they form the practice.

Pillar 01

Enterprise UX & Complex Systems Design

Multi-surface, multi-stakeholder digital products designed for the people who use them every day — not just for the demo. B2B SaaS, internal tools, frontline worker apps, enterprise platforms with high workflow complexity.

Pillar 02

Service Design for Platform Coherence

Mapping the full service ecosystem to close the gap between internal org structure and user experience across touchpoints. The core offer for multi-location enterprises, platform fragmentation, and cross-team alignment problems.

Pillar 03

Regulated & Compliance-Aware UX

Designing financial, healthcare, and enterprise products within compliance constraints without sacrificing usability. Rooted in deep fintech and banking experience. This separates Allfer from design generalists in high-stakes industries.

Pillar 04

AI Product & Transformation Design

Designing for the human side of AI-powered products — the handoffs, the trust moments, the edge cases, and the workflows automation doesn't cover. Grounded in NNG AI Experience Design certification and ThoughtWorks AI initiative experience.
