## The Decision

The company adopts the three-tier engagement model with the following rate structure:

| Engagement | Rate | Structure |
|---|---|---|
| Discovery Sprint | $6,500–$9,500 | Fixed fee, 3–4 weeks |
| Project Engagement | $18,000–$35,000 | Scoped, 6–12 weeks |
| Embedded Retainer | $10,000–$14,000/month | Ongoing, ~60 hrs/month |

Add-on services range from $1,500/day (workshops) to $225/hour (AI consulting).

## Pricing Rationale

**Discovery Sprint at $6,500–$9,500** positions the entry point as accessible relative to the value it delivers (service ecosystem audit, journey mapping, prioritized roadmap). The low end of the range covers 3-week engagements with limited stakeholder access; the high end covers 4-week engagements with more interview rounds.

**Project Engagement at $18,000–$35,000** implies a blended rate of $180–$250/hour across 100–140 hours. This is consistent with senior UX consultant rates at boutique agencies and below Big 4 strategy consulting rates — which is appropriate positioning for a specialized design practice.

**Embedded Retainer at $10,000–$14,000/month** for ~60 hours implies $167–$233/hour fully loaded. This is the most efficient structure for both parties: the client gets continuity and institutional knowledge; the practice gets predictable revenue.

## What Was Validated

- Boutique enterprise design consultancies in comparable markets charge $150–$300/hour for senior talent
- The Discovery Sprint price point is consistent with what Bain/McKinsey charge for a 2-day diagnostic workshop — but Allfer delivers more structured output over a longer horizon
- Retainer pricing is below what large consultancies charge for embedded senior designers ($15,000–$25,000/month) while being above freelancer rates

## Discovery Sprint as Mandatory Entry

The founding team recommends that all new client relationships begin with a Discovery Sprint. Reasons:
1. Scopes subsequent work more accurately — reduces project overrun risk
2. Demonstrates the practice's methodology before larger commitment
3. Delivers standalone value — client cannot lose if they don't continue
4. Builds the relationship and trust required for Project or Retainer work

## Review Point

Rate structure will be reviewed after the first three completed engagements. If win rate on Discovery Sprints is below 60%, pricing may need adjustment. If all clients immediately request Project Engagements after Discovery, that is a signal to raise the Discovery Sprint floor.
