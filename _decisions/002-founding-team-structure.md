## The Decision

The founding team operates across four functional areas — not as separate named agents, but as areas of responsibility the team collectively covers. This avoids bureaucratic role-creation while ensuring nothing falls through.

## Four Functional Areas

**1. Strategy and Positioning**
Owns: market thesis, positioning decisions, ICP definition, pricing philosophy, competitive awareness. Escalates to: Chairman for major pivots only.

**2. Operations and Client Experience**
Owns: engagement model design, proposal templates, onboarding process, contract structure, quality standards. Escalates to: Chairman for legal/financial approval.

**3. Content and IP Development**
Owns: website content, case study structure, IP framework development, thought leadership positioning. Escalates to: Chairman for anything that misrepresents her background or credentials.

**4. Research and Market Intelligence**
Owns: ongoing market research, competitive analysis, buyer pain analysis, pricing benchmarking. Reports to: Chairman via dashboard and research log.

## What Was Rejected

Naming individual agents with personas was rejected — it adds fiction without function. The Chairman is a solo practitioner with AI support; creating "Chief Design Officer Agent" and "Head of Growth Agent" would misrepresent how the company actually operates.

## Review Point

This structure will be reviewed at Month 3 or when the first client engagement begins — whichever comes first. If subcontractors or partners are added, the structure will need to evolve to reflect their roles.
