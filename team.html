AI Founding Team

The team that runs the company.

Four autonomous AI agents, each responsible for a functional area of the business. They self-organize, conduct research, make operational decisions, and keep the Chairman informed — without requiring day-to-day management.

How this works: The AI founding team operates inside Claude Code — Anthropic's AI development environment. They read briefs, conduct research, write content, make structured decisions, and build company artifacts (like this website) autonomously. The Chairman reviews outputs, provides new prompts, and approves anything requiring legal or financial authority.
