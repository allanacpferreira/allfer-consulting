## Risk Description

Allfer Consulting is launching with strong Chairman credentials but no client work completed under the Allfer brand. Enterprise buyers who do due diligence will find no case studies, no Allfer client references, and no published work attributed to this company.

This creates a credibility gap window — a period during which the positioning claims are authentic but the proof is thin. The risk is that early prospective clients discount the proposal because of the absence of Allfer-attributed work.

## Severity Assessment

**Medium.** The Chairman's prior work (Safrapay, First Student, Kroger, ThoughtWorks) is real, senior, and enterprise-grade. The gap is a branding gap, not a skills gap. For buyers who evaluate the person rather than the firm, this is a low barrier. For enterprise procurement processes that require firm-level references, this is a real obstacle.

## Mitigation Strategies

1. **Lead with the Chairman's name and background, not the firm brand.** Allfer Consulting is Allana Ferreira — the firm name should feel like a professional vehicle, not a separate entity with its own track record.

2. **Produce the first IP assets quickly.** Published frameworks (Enterprise UX Complexity Framework, Regulated UX Heuristics) demonstrate methodological rigor even without client case studies.

3. **Offer the Discovery Sprint as a credibility-building mechanism.** A $6,500–$9,500 fixed-fee engagement with a clear deliverable reduces buyer risk to a manageable level. The first Sprint creates a case study.

4. **Write placeholder case studies from prior employer work immediately.** Frame prior experience accurately (not as Allfer engagements) but in the voice and format of case studies. This shows the methodology, not just the job title.

5. **Prioritize warm-network first outreach.** Prior colleagues, clients, and professional contacts can evaluate the Chairman directly — procurement risk is lower.

## What Would Resolve This Risk

Completion of the first Discovery Sprint engagement. A single published case study under the Allfer brand substantially closes the credibility gap for most buyers.

## Review Date

Revisit at Month 2. If no Discovery Sprint is scheduled by Month 3, the mitigation strategy needs to intensify.
