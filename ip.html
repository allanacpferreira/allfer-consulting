IP Roadmap

Building defensible intellectual property.

Reusable frameworks, methodologies, and tools that make the practice more rigorous, scalable, and differentiated over time. IP is how a solo consultancy builds leverage without growing headcount.

IP Strategy Decision

The founding team has decided to prioritize IP that (1) directly supports client delivery, (2) demonstrates methodological rigor to buyers, and (3) can be licensed or productized over time. IP that exists only as marketing collateral is deprioritized.

Priority IP Assets

IP Asset 01 — Priority: High

Enterprise UX Complexity Assessment Framework

In Development

A structured methodology for assessing the complexity, fragmentation, and coherence risk of enterprise digital products. Used internally to scope Discovery Sprints and deliverable Project Engagements. Can be published as a standalone tool.

Supports: Discovery Sprint Supports: Project Engagement Publishable
IP Asset 02 — Priority: High

Regulated Industry UX Heuristics

Planned

An evaluation framework for UX quality in fintech, banking, and healthcare products — accounting for disclosure requirements, compliance constraints, and trust-critical interactions. Directly grounded in Chairman's Safrapay experience.

Supports: UX Audit Supports: Compliance Review Licensable
IP Asset 03 — Priority: Medium

Service Design Audit Methodology

Planned

A repeatable audit process for evaluating service coherence across enterprise platforms. Produces a structured finding set and prioritized remediation roadmap. Designed to be the backbone of the Discovery Sprint deliverable.

Supports: Discovery Sprint Supports: Embedded Retainer
IP Asset 04 — Priority: Medium

Frontline Worker UX Design Patterns

Planned

A documented pattern library for mobile and web applications used by frontline workers — accounting for time pressure, physical context, variable connectivity, and shift-based workflows. Grounded in First Student engagement.

IP Asset 05 — Priority: Medium

AI Product Experience Design Guidelines

Planned

A practitioner's guide to designing the human-AI interaction layer — covering trust calibration, error recovery, handoff design, and automation boundary communication. Grounded in NNG certification and ThoughtWorks AI work.

IP Asset 06 — Priority: Lower

Discovery Sprint Playbook

Planned

A complete internal run-of-show for delivering a Discovery Sprint — templates, facilitation guides, interview frameworks, synthesis methods, and report structure. Enables quality consistency across future partners or subcontractors.
